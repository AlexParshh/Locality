Most Asked Questions

  • Does this site make money?

    Nope. This is simply an inititative to promote local businesses in the area.

  • How do I add my business?

    As long as your business is registered, our service has the capability to feature you depending on your location to a user. If your establishment is close by to a user, your business will be featured as a card on Locality.

  • There is incorrect information?
